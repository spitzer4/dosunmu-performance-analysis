# Ayo Dosunmu Performance Analysis
## 1. Background and Overview
This project aims to analyze Ayo Dosunmu's 2023-24 performance as a key player for the Chicago Bulls.
The goal is to extract meaningful insights about his performance trends over the course of the season by analyzing game-by-game statistics and comparing these to the team's overall performance.

This project uses historical game data, performance metrics, and statistical analysis to provide an overview of Ayo’s contributions to the team's success. The key focus areas are:

- **Player performance metrics:** Points, assists, rebounds, shooting efficiency, and more.
- **Team impact:** How Ayo’s performance correlates with the team’s success (wins vs. losses).

## 4. Insights Deep Dive
**1. Shooting Efficiency**

Dosunmu consistently delivered a field goal percentage averaging 45%, with noticeable improvement in late-season games, suggesting growth and adaptation. While his three-point percentage fluctuated around 33%, his ability to maintain efficiency inside the arc drove his scoring reliability. Strong shooting performances directly correlated with higher game impact, highlighting the importance of refining his shooting mechanics and decision-making.

**2. Scoring Distribution and Trends**

Dosunmu's scoring performance varied significantly, with high-scoring games (20+ points) more frequently occurring at home. This trend underscores the value of leveraging the supportive home environment to maximize offensive output. His consistency at home translated into meaningful contributions to team victories, emphasizing the need to create similar conditions for success during away games.

**3. Three-Point and Free Throw Performance**

While Dosunmu's free throw percentage stood at an impressive 78%, his limited attempts suggest a missed opportunity to capitalize on easy scoring chances. Additionally, inconsistent three-point shooting reduced his ability to stretch defenses effectively. Prioritizing aggression in drawing fouls and improving shooting from deep would elevate both his individual impact and the team's offensive versatility.

**4. Team Impact**

Games where Dosunmu excelled in rebounds, assists, and steals showed a clear positive correlation with team success, as evidenced by heatmap analyses. His contributions across these areas amplified his value beyond scoring, reinforcing the importance of encouraging a well-rounded game. By focusing on multi-metric performance, Dosunmu can enhance both his own growth and the Bulls' chances of winning.

## 5. Recommendations
**1. Enhance Aggression in Driving:**
Encourage Dosunmu to draw more fouls to increase free throw attempts, improving both scoring efficiency and creating additional opportunities for the team.

**2. Targeted Three-Point Training:**
Focus on improving consistency from beyond the arc to diversify offensive capabilities and maximize scoring potential.

**3. Leverage Home Court Advantage:**
Strategize plays to capitalize on Dosunmu’s stronger home-game performance, particularly during critical matchups.

**4. Focus on Multifaceted Contributions:**
Incentivize contributions in rebounds and assists, which have shown to correlate with higher team impact and success rates.
